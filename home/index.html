About Me

Annabel Lian

Welcome to my website for ECE 5160: Fast Robots, where I will be documenting my labs focused on the system-level design and implementation of fast, dynamic autonomous robots. I am currently a Masters of Engineering student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Cornell University. My academic interests lie in embedded systems and robotics. Outside of my academic pursuits, I’m training to be an aerialist and enjoy weight training.
